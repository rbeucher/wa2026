About Antalya

Antalya is one of Turkey’s most loved coastal destinations, right on the Mediterranean. It is famous for its beaches, sunshine, beautiful scenery, waterfalls, historic sites, and great food.

Whether you want to relax, explore the old town (Kaleiçi), do day trips, or just enjoy a long dinner by the sea, it is a wonderful place for a holiday. We really hope you can turn this trip into a special time for yourself as well.

Flights and travel

Because people are coming from all over the world (Australia, Europe, Iran, the USA, and elsewhere), there are many possible routes into Antalya International Airport (AYT).

  • From Australia: We recommend looking at Qatar Airways. It is usually a solid option for timing and connections.
  • From France and Europe: Transavia can be a good option from France, and there are also many flights from major European cities that connect directly to Antalya.
  • Note: Based on our experience, we suggest avoiding Turkish Airlines if you can.

These are just suggestions. Please choose what best suits your budget and schedule.
